Business Support Teams - Help and Advice

 
   

 

 

These are the workshops that we currently offer - if you need help with a problem that is not covered here, then please get in touch with your local Business Support Team.

Online Services

This presentation provides details of the online services currently offered by Inland Revenue.

  • For Employers

Paying Your Employees

We will help you to work out your employees' tax and National Insurance, and give you tips on how to keep good records and where you can turn to for help

Calculating National Insurance Contributions for Directors

We will explain the special rules for paying Directors, along with the higher rates of tax and 'K' codes.

Paying expenses and giving benefits to your employees

This workshop will cover the most common types of expenses and benefits, and we will explain which tax and National Insurance will be due

Statutory Sick Pay (SSP)

We will talk you through how to pay SSP to eligible employees, and what you need to know if you operate your own sick pay scheme

Statutory Pay and Leave for Maternity, Paternity and Adoption( SMP, SPP and SAP)

We will help you with paying SMP, SPP and SAP to eligible employees, what the leave conditions are and with what to do if an employee is not eligible.

Paying Tax Credits

We will talk you through how to pay tax credits to your employees and what records you need to keep.

Collecting Student Loans

We will explain how and when to deduct student loan repayments

End of Year Returns (runs February-April)

We can explain how to complete your End of Year Returns, which forms you need to send to the Inland Revenue and when they are due.

End of Year Expenses and Benefits (runs February-June)

We can help you with filing your End of Year Returns of Expenses and Benefits, and tell you which forms you need to fill in and when.

  • For Businesses

Becoming Self-Employed

We can start you off on the right track by explaining how to register, the basics of tax, National Insurance and VAT, along with what you will need to do and when to send information.

Self-Assessment (SA) for the Self-Employed

Aimed at sole traders and partnerships we can explain how to complete your SA return. We will cover expenses and allowances, the important dates, and how to pay your tax.

  • For the Construction Industry

The Construction Industry Scheme - for contractors and subcontractors

We will talk you through how the ID card and voucher scheme works and how it affects you.

  • For Companies

IR35 - Calculating the deemed payment

If you are affected by the procedures outlined in the Government Press Release IR35, we can explain how you can calculate the payment

Setting up a limited company

We can help you with the basics of forming a limited company, and let you know how to register with Companies House, the Inland Revenue and Customs & Excise.
